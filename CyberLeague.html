CyberLeague

Tactical, team-based FPS focused on skill and strategy

Overview

CyberLeague is a competitive first-person shooter game designed for fast-paced multiplayer action. with no In game purchases or ads, focusing purely on skill-based gameplay. with a variety of game modes and maps, CyberLeague offers an engaging experience for FPS enthusiasts. competitive gameplay, team strategies, and individual skill are key to dominating the arena.

Features

  • No In app purchases
  • Competative gameplay
  • Multiple Game modes
  • Variety of Maps

Development

Development started in unity using C# as the primary programming language. I started making the game in early 2021, focusing on core mechanics and multiplayer functionality. Over time, I refined the gameplay, added new features, and optimized performance to ensure a smooth gaming experience. The game is continuously updated based on player feedback and emerging trends in the FPS genre. we then lost the project files due to a hard drive failure in late 2023. and recently restarted development in mid 2025. this time not on my own but with a small team.
