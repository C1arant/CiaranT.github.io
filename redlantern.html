Red Lantern

Deliver Packages through a cozy japanese town.

Overview

this project was made in unreal engine 5.6 and features a small portion of a japanese city. the player controls a character who must deliver packages to various locations while avoiding obstacles and navigating the environment. the game features simple mechanics, Retro visuals, and a relaxing atmosphere. aswel as a day and night cycle, weather effects, and ambient sounds to enhance the immersive experience.

Features

  • Day Night Cycle
  • Random Package delivery points
  • Cozy Japanese Town Environment
  • Simple Delivery Mechanics
  • Weather system
  • Weight System
