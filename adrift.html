Overview

ADRIFT is a standalone short horror experience a love letter to classic sci-fi horror like Alien, Event Horizon, and Dead Space, told through the lens of isolation, communication, and identity.

Features

  • Deep Space Mystery
  • Immersive Atmosphere
  • Psycologiccal Distortion
  • Fully Voiced Narrative
  • Cinematic Presentation
  • Built for story lovers
