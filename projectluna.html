Project Luna

Play as Hayden Blaze and fight across the universe for the UDC as special forces taking down rebellions and bring peace and security to all who need it.

Overview

Project Luna is an epic adventure game set in a mysterious lunar environment. Players will explore vast landscapes, uncover secrets, and embark on thrilling quests. This section is for a general overview, goals, and what makes it unique.

Features

  • Massive single-player campaign
  • Customisable weapon and space ship
  • Wave based minigame
  • Free post launch updates

Screenshots

Development

Built with Unity Engine. Project Luna has been in the work on and off for many years, with the first mission prototyped in 2023. after that the project was lost due to a hard drive failure. in 2025 I decided to restart the project from scratch and rebuild it with a fresh touch.
